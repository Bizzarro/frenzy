Questions and Common Issues

I just purchased Frenzy but I haven't received my license key.

Check your spam folder. You should normally receive your license details within a minute.
If you still haven't received your license key after a few hours drop us an email.

I lost my license key.

Put your email address in on this page and we'll send it to you again.

My license key isn't working.

Try disabling Little Snitch or any other firewall software that you have running and try again.
If you're still stuck, email us.

Can I use my license key to register Frenzy on multiple Macs?

If you are the user of the license that's fine.

What's your upgrade policy?

Point releases are free updates. Major releases e.g. 2.0, 3.0 will be paid upgrades.
